## Key Findings at a Glance

- Ideal deck length: **10-15 slides**
- Investors spend ~**2-4 minutes** on an emailed deck before deciding on a meeting
- **75% of decks** skip or underdeliver the "Why Now?" slide
- At pre-seed, **team slide** gets 30-40% more VC attention than at later stages
- The **product slide** gets the most time (~77 seconds) of any section
- Three slides carry extra weight in B2B: go-to-market, business model, and traction
